Best Family Board Games

A good family game has a rules footprint that fits on a single page, decisions that feel meaningful to every player at the table, and a play time short enough that nobody checks their phone. The games below clear all three bars.

We’ve deliberately avoided the classic nostalgia trap — Monopoly, Sorry, and Risk are fine, but they’re not in this list because modern designs have simply gotten better at every dimension those games try to hit. Every game here is available on Amazon right now.
