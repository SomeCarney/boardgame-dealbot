About Tabletop Tracker

Tabletop Tracker watches Amazon prices on board games and tabletop games and flags it when something genuinely drops in price — not a "Sale" badge that doesn't mean much, but a real, measurable drop against what a game has actually been selling for over the last few months.

The site is run by one person with an automated price-tracking system doing the watching. New listings get checked every few hours; anything that clears our bar (see How We Pick Deals) gets posted here with a plain description of what changed and why it qualified.

This is a small, independent project, not a retailer and not affiliated with Amazon or any publisher. As an Amazon Associate, this site earns a commission from qualifying purchases made through the links here — that's the only way it's funded, and it doesn't change which deals get posted or how they're described.

If a listed price looks wrong, or a game shouldn't have qualified, that's a bug, not a judgment call — the filtering is mechanical, and mechanical filters occasionally let through something they shouldn't.
