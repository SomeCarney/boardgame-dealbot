Best Strategy Board Games

Strategy games reward thinking ahead, reading opponents, and understanding systems — and the best ones do all three without requiring a PhD to set up or a dedicated game room to store. The games below range from “accessible to anyone” to “will take years to fully solve.”

Play time and complexity are noted for each. If you’re new to hobby strategy games, Wingspan, Cascadia, or Ticket to Ride are excellent starting points. If you want something meatier, Terraforming Mars or 7 Wonders Duel are next steps.
